Info
Landscape

Loamy and rocky descents​,​ traversing down the steep fir​,​ beech and oak woods. Uphills and on double and singletracks on wide open ridges​,​ overlooking Brezno and it's surroundings.

Trail

Mostly manmade technical singletrails with minimal alteration to natural surfaces​,​ no big berms or jumps.

Other

There are 2 mountain huts on the top of 2 mountains that we will pass. We can grab a coffee or beer on a first one. Second one is quite new and has very good slovak cuisine​,​ where we will go for a lunch​,​ just before the final descent.

Services

Shuttle is there every half an hour, but you have to be there 5 min before to buy ticket at station.

Warning

This trail is meant for no noobs, they might die in th process of elimination on trail hunt.
